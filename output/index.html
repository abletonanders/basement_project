7 Years of Basement

2019 - 2026

Hot off a Wire Festival that indulged New York with a 50 hour carry, Basement commences year 7 with a club night that stretches deep into the afternoon. The following month, Wire V introduces Thursday programming featuring exclusively live music. Collaborations with London’s esteemed Fold club, Voices from the Lake and Yanamaste are guaranteed to titillate. Having trouble at the door? As always, check Clinton’s tips on how to show up, show out and party all night long.
